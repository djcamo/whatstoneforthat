Citrine

Abundance and prosperity

Citrine is known as the "Merchant's Stone" because it's associated with success in business and trade. It's believed to bring good fortune and prosperity, and to help people achieve their goals.

Chakras: Solar Plexus
Birthstone Month: November
Zodiac: Aries, Gemini, Leo, Libra
